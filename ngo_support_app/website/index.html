About Beacon of New Beginnings

Founded with a mission to transform lives and restore hope

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support, safety, and empowerment opportunities for survivors of domestic violence, abuse, and homelessness in Ghana, fostering healing and sustainable independence.

Our Vision

A Ghana where every survivor of abuse and homelessness has access to safety, healing resources, and opportunities for empowerment, leading to thriving, independent lives within supportive communities.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Creating secure environments for healing
  • Dignity & Respect: Honoring each person's worth and choices
  • Empowerment: Building skills and confidence for independence
  • Community: Fostering supportive relationships and networks
  • Confidentiality: Protecting privacy and personal information
